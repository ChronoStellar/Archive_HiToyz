About Us

Established in 2019, HI-Toyz is a beloved toy store offering plastic model kits and figure statues. We cater to local collectors, provide a diverse selection of toys, and serve as an activity center. As an official reseller for top brands like Tamiya, Bandai, and Mattel, we bring joy to toy enthusiasts.
